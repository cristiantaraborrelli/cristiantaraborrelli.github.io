The Paralympic flame is extinguished. A child blows. An arena holds its breath.

On March 15, 2026, *Italian Souvenir* closed the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games at the Cortina Curling Olympic Stadium — broadcast worldwide to hundreds of millions of viewers.

My contribution to the ceremony covered three roles: Content Supervisor, Show Caller and Scenic Design. I worked on the show's dramaturgy alongside the creative team and directed the live execution from the floor on the night.

The scenic element I authored — the *Boule de Neige* — became the ceremony's most recognisable image: a monumental luminous sphere enclosing the Duomo di Milano and the Dolomites within a single volume. When Sofia, the young protagonist, approached the sphere and blew out the Paralympic flame, the arena held its breath.

Three acts built together with an exceptional team:
— Dergin Tokmak (Cirque du Soleil): luminous crutches tracing the meaning of Paralympic sport
— Formisanoff's cello and Thomas Signorelli's choreography dissolving bodies into the Alpine landscape
— Planet Funk closing the Games in music and light

Artistic direction & show direction: Angelo Bonello. Creative direction: Francesco Paolo Conticello. Production: G2 Eventi · @castadivagroup.
