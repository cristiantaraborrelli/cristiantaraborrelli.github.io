LA JURA — TEATRO LIRICO DI CAGLIARI, 2015

A work that waited fifty-six years for its own redress.

Gavino Gabriel — pioneer of Italian ethnomusicology, first director of the Discoteca di Stato — writes La Jura from 1905 to 1959. He rewrites it, abandons it, returns to it. In 1959, during rehearsals at Cagliari, the conductor Dante Ullu tears pages from the score. Wounded, Gabriel rewrites the entire opera in a single summer. The version of 18 September 1959 is not a variant: it is a response to a betrayal. When he dies in 1980, that version has never been performed.

Its first integral performance: 2015, @teatroliricodicagliari, in the critical edition by Susanna Pasticci, author of the essay «Sperimentare la tradizione». Pasticci does not retrieve: she establishes. Without her, La Jura would have remained a phantom.

I signed, for the first time, direction, sets and costumes together. La Jura cannot bear division. It refuses verismo — Mascagni, Leoncavallo — and summons an elsewhere: an ethnographic-modernist device where characters do not act, they are acted upon by the places.

Angela Buscemi's costumes — t'agus, fazzolettoni, Gallurese embroideries — are philology worn on the body. They do not cite, they refound. The lighting of Guido Levi, no longer with us, sculpted the stage with the discipline of one who treats light as thought. The privilege was mine. The video-scenography of Fabio Massimo Iaquone (@fabio_massimo_iaquone) and Luca Attilii (@luca_attilii) found here its purest form: the closing loom of nine and a half kilometres of elastic threads, an explicit citation of Maria Lai.

Sandro Sanna conducted. Antonella Agati signed the choreography. The Accademia Popolare Gallurese carried the tàsgia chant.

Bruno Di Marino (@b.dima1966), in his essay «Nel cuore del Novecento», closes with the line that has defined for me the meaning of the entire operation:

«We have finally entered the heart of the twentieth century.»

La Jura was not ours. It had belonged to Gabriel, to the archive, to Sardinia. For two weeks of November we held it in custody.
