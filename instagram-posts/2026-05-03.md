LA JURA — TEATRO LIRICO DI CAGLIARI, 2015

A work that waited fifty-six years for its own redress.

GAVINO GABRIEL — pioneer of Italian ethnomusicology, first director of the Discoteca di Stato — writes La Jura from 1905 to 1959. He rewrites, abandons, returns to it. In 1959, during rehearsals at Cagliari, the conductor Dante Ullu tears pages from the score. Gabriel rewrites the entire opera in a single summer. The version of 18 September 1959 is not a variant: it is 𝗮 𝗿𝗲𝘀𝗽𝗼𝗻𝘀𝗲 𝘁𝗼 𝗮 𝗯𝗲𝘁𝗿𝗮𝘆𝗮𝗹. When he dies in 1980, that version has never been performed.

Its first integral performance: 2015, @teatroliricodicagliari, in the critical edition by SUSANNA PASTICCI (@susannapasticci), author of the essay «Sperimentare la tradizione». Pasticci does not retrieve: she establishes. Without her, La Jura would have remained a phantom.

I signed, for the first time, direction, sets and costumes together. La Jura cannot bear division. It refuses verismo and summons an elsewhere: an ethnographic-modernist device where characters do not act, they are acted upon by the places.

ANGELA BUSCEMI's costumes — t'agus, fazzolettoni — are philology worn on the body. They do not cite, they refound. The lighting of GUIDO LEVI, no longer with us, sculpted the stage with the discipline of one who treats light as thought. The privilege was mine. The video-scenography of FABIO MASSIMO IAQUONE (@fabio_massimo_iaquone) and LUCA ATTILII (@luca_attilii) found here its purest form: the closing loom of nine kilometres of elastic threads, citation of Maria Lai.

Sandro Sanna conducting. Antonella Agati choreography. Accademia Popolare Gallurese for the tàsgia chant.

Cast: @rubenspelizzari, @gtalamo80, @paolettamarro__, Caruso, Lentini, Pierpaoli, Ebau.

BRUNO DI MARINO (@b.dima1966), in his essay «Nel cuore del Novecento», closes with the line that has defined the meaning of the entire operation:

«We have finally entered the heart of the twentieth century.»

La Jura was not ours. It had belonged to Gabriel, to the archive, to Sardinia. For two weeks of November 𝘄𝗲 𝗵𝗲𝗹𝗱 𝗶𝘁 𝗶𝗻 𝗰𝘂𝘀𝘁𝗼𝗱𝘆.
