**MERAVIGLIOSO.**

Fashion. Music. Theatre. The three languages of Italian beauty — performed live, for 800 guests.

Palazzo del Ghiaccio, Milan. May 7th, 2024.

When Amway China commissioned a gala to celebrate its top sales force, they wanted more than an evening. They wanted Italy — its elegance, its drama, its irreducible splendour.

We built *Meraviglioso* as a theatrical opera in three acts: each dedicated to one expression of Made in Italy. Fashion. Music. Theatre aesthetics. The stage at Palazzo del Ghiaccio became a living canvas — étoile Virna Toppi dancing through a world of light and fabric, while Maestro Beppe Vessicchio conducted a symphonic orchestra into silence and then into fire. 800 guests. One night. No recording could hold what the room contained.

Artistic direction carries this specific weight: not just the logistics of a grand spectacle, but the intelligence of sequence — the decision that fashion comes first, that silence before music is structural, that the final image must burn long after the lights go out.

*Meraviglioso* won three awards at BEA Italia 2024: Silver for B2I Event, Silver ex aequo for Celebration / Anniversary, Bronze for Incentive / Team Building.

This is what live events can be when they are treated as art.
