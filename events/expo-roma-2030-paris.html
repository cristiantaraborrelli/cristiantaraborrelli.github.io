Thematic Symposium Expo 2030 Rome: Inclusion and Sustainability at the Palais Brongniart in Paris

The Thematic Symposium of Expo 2030 Rome, held on October 11, 2023, at the prestigious Palais Brongniart in Paris, was a key event in the bid of Rome to host Expo 2030. Organized in collaboration with Filmmaster Events, the symposium brought together international experts, artists, and leaders to address future challenges under the central theme “People and Territories.”

The event, hosted by Mia Ceran, featured high-profile speakers, including:

  • Dimitri Kerkentzes, Secretary General of the BIE
  • Roberto Gualtieri, Mayor of Rome
  • Eric Pulier, entrepreneur and innovator
  • Licypriya Kangujam, environmental activist

The discussions revolved around five key topics:

  1. Living and habitat
  2. Roads, pathways, networks, borders
  3. Territories and inclusion
  4. Environment, energy, resources
  5. Risk, uncertainty, vulnerability, and resilience

Symposium Thématique Expo 2030 Rome : Inclusion et Durabilité au Palais Brongniart à Paris

Le Symposium Thématique d’Expo 2030 Rome, qui s’est tenu le 11 octobre 2023 au prestigieux Palais Brongniart à Paris, a été un événement clé dans la candidature de Rome pour accueillir l’Expo 2030. Organisé en collaboration avec Filmmaster Events, le symposium a réuni des experts internationaux, des artistes et des dirigeants pour aborder les défis futurs sous le thème central « People and Territories ».

L’événement, animé par Mia Ceran, a accueilli des intervenants de haut niveau, notamment :

  • Dimitri Kerkentzes, Secrétaire Général du BIE
  • Roberto Gualtieri, Maire de Rome
  • Eric Pulier, entrepreneur et innovateur
  • Licypriya Kangujam, activiste environnementale

Les discussions ont porté sur cinq thèmes clés :

  1. Vivre et habitat
  2. Routes, chemins, réseaux, frontières
  3. Territoires et inclusion
  4. Environnement, énergie, ressources
  5. Risque, incertitude, vulnérabilité et résilience

Roberto Bolle & Nicoletta Manni

The evening reached its emotional peak with three extraordinary dance performances by Roberto Bolle, Étoile of Teatro alla Scala and American Ballet Theatre, and Nicoletta Manni, Prima Ballerina Étoile of Teatro alla Scala.

Sphere — A declaration of love for the planet and an urgent appeal to care for it. Conceived by Bolle with choreographer Mauro Bigonzetti and accompanied by original music from violinist Alessandro Quarta, Bolle lifts a large sphere symbolizing our fragile planet, inviting the audience to embrace and protect it.

Waves — A fusion of dance and technology offering a futuristic perspective. With music by Davide Di Leo (Boosta) dialoguing with Bolle’s movement, in a choreography by Massimiliano Volpini.

Caravaggio — Choreographed by Mauro Bigonzetti, set to music by Claudio Monteverdi arranged by Bruno Moretti, light becomes the protagonist — shaping the lines and bodies of Roberto Bolle and Nicoletta Manni, transforming the humble subjects of Caravaggio’s works into figures of grace and eternal beauty.

La serata ha raggiunto il suo apice emotivo con tre straordinarie performance di danza di Roberto Bolle, Étoile del Teatro alla Scala e dell’American Ballet Theatre, e Nicoletta Manni, Prima Ballerina Étoile del Teatro alla Scala.

Sphere — Una dichiarazione d’amore per il pianeta e un appello urgente a prendersene cura. Ideata da Bolle con il coreografo Mauro Bigonzetti e accompagnata dalle musiche originali del violinista Alessandro Quarta, Bolle solleva una grande sfera che simboleggia il nostro fragile pianeta, invitando il pubblico ad abbracciarlo e proteggerlo.

Waves — Una fusione di danza e tecnologia che offre una prospettiva futuristica. Con musiche di Davide Di Leo (Boosta) in dialogo con il movimento di Bolle, in una coreografia di Massimiliano Volpini.

Caravaggio — Coreografato da Mauro Bigonzetti, su musiche di Claudio Monteverdi arrangiate da Bruno Moretti, la luce diventa protagonista — modellando le linee e i corpi di Roberto Bolle e Nicoletta Manni, trasformando i soggetti umili delle opere di Caravaggio in figure di grazia e bellezza eterna.

A Major Step Forward for Rome's Bid

The Thematic Symposium of Expo 2030 Rome was a pivotal moment in Rome’s bid, demonstrating the city’s ability to tackle global issues through constructive dialogues and high-level cultural events. The event reinforced Rome’s position as an ideal host for Expo 2030, emphasizing its values of inclusion, sustainability, and innovation.
