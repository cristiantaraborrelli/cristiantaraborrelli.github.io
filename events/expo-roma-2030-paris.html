Expo Roma 2030
in Paris

Palais Brongniart · Paris · October 11, 2023

The Thematic Symposium of Expo 2030 Rome, held on October 11, 2023 at the prestigious Palais Brongniart in Paris, was a key event in Rome's bid to host Expo 2030. Organized in collaboration with Filmmaster Events, the symposium brought together international experts, artists, and leaders to address future challenges under the central theme "People and Territories."

Hosted by Mia Ceran, the event featured high-profile speakers including Dimitri Kerkentzes, Secretary General of the Bureau International des Expositions. Cristian Taraborrelli contributed to the scenic and visual concept of the evening.
