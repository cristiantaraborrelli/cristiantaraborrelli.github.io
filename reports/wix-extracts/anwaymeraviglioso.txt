EVENTO: AMWAY CHINA — MERAVIGLIOSO
Tagline Wix: "Where Art Meets Elegance: An Unforgettable Celebration of Italian Heritage"
Subtitle: Amway China Gala Dinner — meraviglioso
DATA: 7 maggio 2024
LOCATION: Palazzo del Ghiaccio, Milano

DESCRIZIONE WIX:
"Meraviglioso" was an extraordinary gala crafted exclusively for Amway, seamlessly blending the elegance and artistic brilliance of Italian culture. Hosted at the prestigious Palazzo del Ghiaccio, the event mesmerized 800 guests with an unforgettable evening celebrating the achievements of Amway China's top sales force.

Under the visionary artistic direction of Cristian Taraborrelli, the gala embodied the essence of Made in Italy through a breathtaking fusion of fashion, music, and theatre. Captivating performances by étoile Virna Toppi and a symphonic orchestra conducted by Maestro Beppe Vessicchio transformed the night into a symphony of Italian elegance, innovation, and pure emotion.

ARTISTIC CONCEPT AND STORYTELLING:
"A Symphony of Italian Excellence which combines the beauty of fashion, the emotion of music, and the drama of theatre. This gala is a tribute to Italy's cultural magnificence."
