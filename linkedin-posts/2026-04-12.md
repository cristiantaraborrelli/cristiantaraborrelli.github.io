## Account: Cristian Taraborrelli

---

*"Meraviglioso"* was built around a question that appears simple and isn't: how do you make 800 people feel, simultaneously, that they are witnessing something that cannot be repeated?

Amway China commissioned this gala for their top sales force at Palazzo del Ghiaccio, Milan — May 2024. The brief asked for Made in Italy. We answered with a theatrical opera in three acts: fashion, music, and theatrical aesthetics as three distinct but inseparable expressions of Italian excellence.

Étoile Virna Toppi and a symphonic orchestra conducted by Maestro Beppe Vessicchio were not embellishments. They were the argument. The production logic was strictly dramaturgical — sequence, tension, release. The same tools we use in opera, applied to a corporate event.

The result: three BEA Italia 2024 awards and, more importantly, a room that fell quiet in the way that only happens when something real is at stake.

For anyone commissioning large-scale events — whether institutional galas, product launches, or fashion presentations — the lesson is the same: the form of the evening is the message.
